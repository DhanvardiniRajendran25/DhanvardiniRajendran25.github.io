The Roadmap

My journey, shipped like a product.

From a small town in Tamil Nadu to building AI products in Boston. Every stop a milestone, every milestone a lesson in what makes products and people thrive.

Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu

Where it started

Born and raised in Thanjavur. Topped school at 95% and served as School Pupil Leader, my first taste of leading people toward a shared goal.

Product mindset: leadership is just listening closely, then delivering what people actually need.

2018 to 2022 · SASTRA University

Engineer first

B.Tech in Computer Science. Discovered product design in my junior year and was hooked. Graduated with four offers: Cognizant, IBM, Deloitte, and Optum.

Product mindset: understand how things are built, and you can imagine what to build next.

2022 to 2024 · Optum, UnitedHealth Group (UHG)

Intern to Product Manager

Joined as an intern, earned a pre-placement offer into product, and was promoted to Product Manager. Owned AI discovery flows for 5M+ members and mentored 50+ new hires. Recognized three times.

Product mindset: earn trust with outcomes, then scale what works to millions.

2024 to 2026 · Northeastern University

Going deeper on AI

MS in Information Systems, Boston. Led a 1,500+ community of aspiring PMs, ran hackathons for 400+ teams, and earned Northeastern's top honors.

Product mindset: the fastest way to master product is to build the community that teaches it.

Now

Building what's next

Shipping AI and agentic products, and looking for the team where I can do my best work.

Product mindset: the next problem worth solving is the one users feel most.
